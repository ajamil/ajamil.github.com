Asif Jamil, Ph.D.

I'm a research scientist, working in the area of human neuroimaging and non-invasive neuromodulation at Massachusetts General Hospital & Harvard Medical School (Boston, USA).

As a member of Joan Camprodon's lab, my research area covers the development and design of novel non-invasive brain stimulation protocols that use electric and magnetic waveforms (tACS, TMS) to interact with the brain's natural oscillations (EEG). These techqniues may offer a new and promising prospect in the development of treatments for neuropsychiatric disorders.
